# 🏢 Executive Workforce Analytics Dashboard | SQL • Python • Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

The Executive Workforce Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end HR Analytics solution designed to analyze workforce demographics, employee attrition, workforce risk factors, and productivity metrics.

The project leverages SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw employee data into actionable business insights that support workforce planning, employee retention strategies, and performance management.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to understand the factors influencing employee attrition, workforce productivity, and employee engagement.

High attrition can lead to:

* Increased recruitment costs
* Loss of organizational knowledge
* Reduced workforce productivity
* Operational disruptions
* Lower employee morale

Management requires a centralized analytics solution to monitor workforce health, identify attrition risks, and support data-driven decision-making.

# 🔍 Key Insights

* Attrition Rate stands at approximately 20%.
* Employees working overtime exhibit higher turnover risk.
* Job satisfaction significantly impacts employee retention.
* Delayed promotions contribute to workforce attrition.
* Training and productivity indicators vary across departments.
* Workforce performance remains relatively stable across the organization.
